About

Question & Ashton is a weekly podcast by Ashton Kemerling dedicated to talking about the history and technology of everything. Ashton is a bit of a “know it all” that has decided to turn his abilities for good, educating you about practically everything.

The podcast is laid out in a series format, with each series covering a single subject. Within each series there will be a number of episodes, each dedicated to a small portion of the topic, like a particular inventor, time period, or invention.

The official discussion for this podcast will be on Reddit at the questionandashton subreddit. There will be an official post for each episode there.
